About Me

Hello! My name is Caroline and I am a freelance copywriter and web designer,based in the north west of England. I have an undergraduate degree in English Literature (BA Hons) and a postgraduate degree in Law.


For many year I spent weekends selling my handmade items at a Makers Market's in and around Manchester. I even went as far as to establish my own makers market in Rossendale on Rawtenstall market for a short period of time. whilst working full time in the health care service, creativity is what I come back to again and again and I am excited to have found a career path that supports my creativity.
